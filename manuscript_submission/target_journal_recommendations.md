## Top Three Practical Targets

1. **Clinical Neurophysiology Practice**: best balance of EEG expertise and methodological/pragmatic scope.
2. **Neurotrauma Reports**: best disease-domain fit for mTBI and honest negative/feasibility findings.
3. **PLOS ONE**: safest broad outlet for technically sound negative public-data work.

## Suggested Submission Strategy

Lead with **Clinical Neurophysiology Practice** if the authors want a clinical EEG readership. Lead with **Neurotrauma Reports** if the priority is mTBI domain visibility. Use **PLOS ONE** if the priority is acceptance of a rigorous negative study with transparent public-data methods.
