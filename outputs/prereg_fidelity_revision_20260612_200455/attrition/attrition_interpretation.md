# Attrition Interpretation

The descriptive completer/non-completer comparison was completed for available baseline clinical variables. Because the locked plan did not define a numeric imbalance threshold for triggering IPW, no inverse-probability-weighted model was added post hoc. EEG predictors could not be compared for non-completers from local processed outputs because their raw S1 EEG was not downloaded or processed in the frozen analytic cohort.
